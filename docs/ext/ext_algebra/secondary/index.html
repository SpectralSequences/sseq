The secondary ($d_2$) layer of ExtAlgebra.

SecondaryExtAlgebra composes an ExtAlgebra with the secondary resolutions of M and the unit k, and exposes:

  • the secondary differential d2 (and the survival check survives),
  • the $E_3$-page data page_data, and
  • the $\Mod_{C\lambda^2}$ secondary product secondary_multiply_into.

These wrap SecondaryResolution and [SecondaryResolutionHomomorphism]; no new linear algebra is implemented here. The layer is split out from ExtAlgebra because the secondary machinery requires CC::Algebra: PairAlgebra, a bound the primary layer does not impose.
