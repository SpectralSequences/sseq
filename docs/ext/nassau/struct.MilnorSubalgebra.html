Expand description

A Milnor subalgebra to be used in Nassauโs algorithm. This is equipped with an ordering of the signature as in Lemma 2.4 of the paper.

To simplify implementation, we pick the ordering so that the (reverse) lexicographic ordering in Lemma 2.4 is just the (reverse) lexicographic ordering of the P parts. This corresponds to the ordering of $\mathcal{P}$ where $P^s_t < P^{sโ}_t$ if $s < sโ$).
